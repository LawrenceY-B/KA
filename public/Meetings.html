Monday Lizalex Meeting

Time: 7pm

Venue: Legon(Lizalex)


The Monday Lizalex meeting is an amazing time of fellowship where members and non members of Kingdom Ambassadors who are on Legon campus meet at Lizalex in Legon to be taught the word bt Commander El. This meeting is held every Monday unless directed otherwise by Commander.COuntless testimonies have come from these meetings and till now it continues to be a blessing not only to KA but to the Legon Compus as a whole.

Linger Long

TAG Meeting

Time: 10am

Venue: K.A Glory Embassy


TAG is an abbreviation for, The Ambassadors Gathering.This is a meeting held on the last Saturday of every month. It is one meeting no one should ever miss, not implying that the other meetings can be missed. At TAG meetings we receive teaching, we pray, we have an awesome family time. The experience at this meeting always leaves people speechless. The dress code for this meeting is official unless directed otherwise by Commander El.
