The Mission


Kingdom Ambassadors is a non-denominational youth movement that began in the year 2019.This movement was formed with the aim of gathering the masses for Christ and raising radical believers who will take the kingdom of God and it's beliefs and principles and shamelessly spread it abroad.Another major vision for the move is to bring out the hidden potentials and gifts in people and train them to exploit it and use it for the glory of the Kingdom. We are on a unique mission in the body of Christ.Though the movement is a youth movement, adults also manage to join us in fellowship'

Leadership


The leadership structure of Kingdom Ambassadors is as follows.The leadership heirarchy begins with the founder, Commander El. After Commander El there are 8 leaders who are referred to as spearheads.These spearheads are each in charge of units in KA called Tribes. KA is made up of 8 Tribes, namely: Commander Tribe,Eagle Tribe, Lion Tribe, Spirit Tribe, Judah Tribe, Zion Tribe, Mystery Tribe(For KNUST). The whole of KA is divided into these tribes.Under each tribe there are 12 Apostles and anyone in the move who shepherds' at least 3 people is known as a captain.

Teams


Kingdom Ambassadors aslo has teams in charge of various aspects of the move. There is the Vision Team in charge of visual communication and graphic design, The Expression Team which is in charge of artwork and crafts and interior design. Rhema Khinisi which works on Animations. Together these teams are called CAT(Creative Arts Team). Some other teams are The Stallions, who are in charge of busing and transportation. There are also teams such as Pneuma Kinisi and Ministers Of Glory who are in charge of dance and music respectively.
